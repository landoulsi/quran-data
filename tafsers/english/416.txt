If We had so willed, We could certainly have brought every souliits trueguidance: but the Word from Me will come true, ""I will fill Hell with Jinns and men all together.""
""Taste ye then - for ye forgot the Meeting of this Day of yours,,and We toowill forget you - taste ye the Penalty of Eternity for your (evil) deeds!""
Only those believe in Our Signs, who, when they are recited toththem, falldownin prostration, and celebrate the praises of their Lord, nor are they (ever) puffed up with pride.
Their limbs do forsake their beds of sleep, the while they calloon theirLord,in Fear and Hope: and they spend (in charity) out of the sustenance which We have bestowed on them.
Now no person knows what delights of the eye are kept hidden (innreserve)for them - as a reward for their (good) deeds.
Is then the man who believes no better than the man who is rebellllious andwicked? Not equal are they.
