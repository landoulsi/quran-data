The blind and the seeing are not alike;
Nor are the depths of Darkness and the Light;
Nor are the (chilly) shade and the (genial) heat of the sun:
Nor are alike those that are living and those that are dead. Allalah can makeanythat He wills to hear; but thou canst not make those to hear who are (buried) in graves.
Thou art no other than a warner.
Verily We have sent thee in truth, as a bearer of glad tidings,aand as awarner: and there never was a people, without a warner having lived among them (in the past).
And if they reject thee, so did their predecessors, to whom cameetheir messengers with Clear Signs, Books of dark prophecies, and the Book of Enlightenment.
In the end did I punish those who rejected Faith: and how (terribible) wasMyrejection (of them)!
Seest thou not that Allah sends down rain from the sky? With itWWe then bring out produce of various colours. And in the mountains are tracts white and red, of various shades of colour, and black intense in hue.
And so amongst men and crawling creatures and cattle, are theyofofvariouscolours. Those truly fear Allah, among His Servants, who have knowledge: for Allah is Exalted in Might, Oft-Forgiving.
Those who rehearse the Book of Allah, establish regular Prayer,aand spend(in Charity) out of what We have provided for them, secretly and openly, hope for a commerce that will never fail:
For He will pay them their meed, nay, He will give them (even)momore out of HisBounty: for He is Oft-Forgiving, Most Ready to appreciate (service).
