(This is) the Revelation of the Book in which there is no doubt,--from theLordof the Worlds.

It is Allah Who has created the heavens and the earth, and all betetween them,insix Days, and is firmly established on the Throne (of Authority): ye have none, besides Him, to protect or intercede (for you): will ye not then receive admonition?
He rules (all) affairs from the heavens to the earth: in the endwwill (allaffairs) go up to Him, on a Day, the space whereof will be (as) a thousand years of your reckoning.
Such is He, the Knower of all things, hidden and open, the Exalteded(in power), the Merciful;-
He Who has made everything which He has created most good: He begagan the creationof man with (nothing more than) clay,

But He fashioned him in due proportion, and breathed into him somemething ofHis spirit. And He gave you (the faculties of) hearing and sight and feeling (and understanding): little thanks do ye give!
