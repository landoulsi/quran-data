O mankind! fear your Lord! for the convulsion of the Hour (of Jududgment) willbea thing terrible!
The Day ye shall see it, every mother giving suck shall forgetheher suckling-babe, and every pregnant female shall drop her load (unformed): thou shalt see mankind as in a drunken riot, yet not drunk: but dreadful will be the Wrath of Allah.
And yet among men there are such as dispute about Allah, withouttknowledge,andfollow every evil one obstinate in rebellion!
About the (Evil One) it is decreed that whoever turns to him forrfriendship,him will he lead astray, and he will guide him to the Penalty of the Fire.
