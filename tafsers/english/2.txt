Alif, Lam, Mim.
This is the Book; in it is guidance sure, without doubt, to thosesewho fear Allah;
Who believe in the Unseen, are steadfast in prayer, and spend oututof whatWehave provided for them;
And who believe in the Revelation sent to thee, and sent beforetthy time,and (in their hearts) have the assurance of the Hereafter.
They are on (true) guidance, from their Lord, and it is these whohowill prosper.
