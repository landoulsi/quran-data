O Ye who believe! Put not yourselves forward before Allah and HisMessenger; but fear Allah: for Allah is He Who hears and knows all things.
O ye who believe! Raise not your voices above the voice of the Prophet, nor speak aloud to him in talk, as ye may speak aloud to one another, lest your deeds become vain and ye perceive not.
Those that lower their voices in the presence of Allah's Messenger,- theirhearts has Allah tested for piety: for them is Forgiveness and a great Reward.
